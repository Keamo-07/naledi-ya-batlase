Who we are

Built by the community, for learner success.

NYB exists for the learner who is trying hard, falling behind, and needs someone patient enough to help the work finally make sense.

NYB was born in this community. We know what it feels like to sit in a classroom and not understand, and to have no one nearby who can help. We know what it costs a family to afford extra lessons. We know the weight of falling behind when you are trying your hardest.

"NYB exists because we refused to let that be anyone else's story."

Founded in Makolokwe Village in 2016, Naledi Ya Batlase serves Grade 8-12 learners across the Bakwena region, Bojanala District and Madibeng Local Municipality. At R5 per session, no learner is turned away because of money.

We focus on the subjects that matter most: improving marks in Mathematics and Physical Science, building confidence across all subjects, and making sure every learner leaves understanding more than when they arrived.

Every star shines differently. Our work is to help each learner find their own light.
