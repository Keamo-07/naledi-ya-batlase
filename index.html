Who we are

We Know What It Costs to Almost Not Make It

NYB was born in this community. We know what it feels like to sit in a classroom and not understand — and to have no one nearby who can help. We know what it costs a family to afford extra lessons. We know the weight of falling behind when you're trying your hardest.

"NYB exists because we refused to let that be anyone else's story."

Founded in Makolokwe Village in 2016, Naledi Ya Batlase was never meant to stay small. We serve Grade 8–12 learners across the Bakwena region, Bojanala District, Madibeng Local Municipality. At R5 per session, no learner is turned away because of money.

That is why we focus on the subjects that matter most — improving marks in Maths and Physical Science, building confidence across all subjects, and making sure every learner walks out of a session understanding more than when they walked in.

Every star shines differently. Our job is to help each learner find their own light.

How we teach

What NYB Stands For

Three principles. Every session. Every tutor. Every learner.

I
We Don't Move On Until It Clicks
No learner gets left behind because the session ran out of time. We pace to understanding, not the clock.
II
No Shame in Struggling
Questions are welcome. Confusion is normal. The only wrong move is leaving without asking. Every tutor at NYB knows that.
III
Every Learner Is Different
We meet each learner where they are — not where the syllabus says they should be. That's how real progress happens.

"I have sat with problems that took longer than anyone expected. I did not stop. That is the only thing I teach that no textbook covers — and it is the most important lesson any learner will ever learn."

— Keamogetswe M. · Founder, Naledi Ya Batlase
